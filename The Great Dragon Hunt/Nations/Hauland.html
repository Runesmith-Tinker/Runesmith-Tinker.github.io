Grand Duchy of Hauland

"Utilitarianism made manifest... at least on the surface. On the outside the roads of their cities look straight and narrow, their clothing bland while serving its purpose, and their buildings unpainted and basic. Yet there is an elegant beauty that can be found in the designs, with still finding ways to not make things unpleasant to look at, while still not being frivolous. If not for the early morning noise, I'd say it'd be quite the pleasant place to live."
