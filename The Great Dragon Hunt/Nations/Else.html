Free Cities of Else

"Ah, the free cities. Their ivory spires are and bright clothes make the journey to see them worth it. While there I'd recommend giving the fish a try, as they have some of the freshest around. Honestly, if it weren't for this simply being the start of my journey, and the white buildings hurting my eyes, I'd potentially have considered spending longer. The people are lovely, the sights a delight, and the libraries... I could lose days exploring them."
