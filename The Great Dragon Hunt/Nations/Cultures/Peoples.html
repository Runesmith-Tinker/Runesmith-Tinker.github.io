The Great Dragon Hunt was written as a setting for a Pathfinder 2e campain. As such, all races below are at least somewhat connected to their PF2e counter part, and will have a link to their Archives of Nethys counterpart.
Potentially of more importance, what is below are simply the most common beliefs and views of each race of people. None of them are truely without exception, especially after hundreds of years for races and cultures to intermingle and interact.
